Netflix Original Film IMDB Score Visualization

 

Created by Jatin Saxena for CS416 Data Visualization at UIUC

The Dataset

The dataset contains all Netflix original films released as of June 1st, 2021, and includes their title, genre, premier date, runtime, IMDB score, and language.

The purpose of this visualization is to explore the following question: has Netflix improved at making original films over time?

Number of Netflix Originals by Year

This graph demonstrates how, over the previous few years, the number of Netflix originals has grown annually. Even though it appears that fewer originals were produced in 2021, Netflix is actually on schedule to generate a similar number of originals to 2020, given the dataset contains originals up to June 1st, 2021.

The data show that Netflix is investing more and more on original movies, but the question of whether the movies are getting better over time still remains. To get closer to an answer, let's next look at the typical IMDB ratings for each year.

Average IMDB Score of Netflix Originals by Year

Action Adventure Animation Anime Christmas Comedy Crime Documentary Drama Fantasy Family Musical Mystery Other Romance Science Fiction Sports film War Western

This graph demonstrates that, starting in 2016, average IMDB ratings have actually been falling over the course of the last few years. The 2021 Netflix original movies have thus far achieved the lowest ratings since 2014. To observe how the scores have changed for a certain collection of genres, check or uncheck the checkboxes.

This graph finally provides an answer to the question of whether Netflix has become more adept at creating original movies: on the whole, they haven't. Despite this, it's challenging to determine how each genre fared for a particular year. Let's examine the average ratings for each genre next (and lastly) to gain even more understanding of the data.

Average IMDB Score of Netflix Originals by Genre

2014 2015 2016 2017 2018 2019 2020 2021

This graph demonstrates how well Netflix does while making original animated, crime, documentary, and war movies. To view the scores for each genre for a specific period of years, check or uncheck the boxes.

According to the last three charts, Netflix is generating an increasing number of original movies, but the quality of these movies has reportedly dropped over the previous few years. However, some genres have unquestionably outpaced others.
